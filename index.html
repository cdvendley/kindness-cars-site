How it started

Kindness Cars began with Fisher, a young boy who loves Hot Wheels and Matchbox cars. Because of a medical condition, he visits Children’s Hospital every few months. On those visits, he started bringing brand-new toy cars to share with other kids in the waiting room.

What we do

We collect and gift brand-new toy cars to children during hospital visits, helping create a moment of comfort, distraction, and joy for kids and families navigating hard days.

Start your own gifting practice

We encourage others to start their own gifting tradition when they go to the hospital. Bring a few new toy cars, share them with children in waiting areas (when permitted), and invite your community to do the same.
