Fisher’s Story

Kindness Cars began with Fisher, a young boy who loves Hot Wheels and Matchbox cars. Because of a medical condition, he visits Children’s Hospital every few months. On those visits, he started bringing brand-new toy cars to share with other kids in the waiting room.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bring comfort, distraction, and joy to children during hospital visits by sharing small gifts that can make a big difference.

How to Help

You can support Kindness Cars by donating new toy cars, helping fund purchases, or spreading the word in your community.
